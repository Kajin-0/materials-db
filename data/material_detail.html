Gallium Arsenide (GaAs)

III-V Semiconductor | Photonics | Electronics

Description

Gallium Arsenide (GaAs) is a III-V compound semiconductor with a direct bandgap, high electron mobility, and excellent optoelectronic properties. It is widely used in infrared LEDs, laser diodes, and high-frequency electronics.
